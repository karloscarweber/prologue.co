Danny wanted to learn to do what I do.

My Sister Danny said that if I can learn to program and make apps, then she can do it too. So she ponied up the dough for a little MacBook Air and started studying. Together We started Prologue productions so that we could publish apps together.

Swift is a brand new programming language from apple, everyone is a beginner with swift. There aren’t any books teaching Swift for beginners, So we wrote the very first book.

Together we offer two perspectives, One from an experienced programmer, Another from a complete beginner. This gives the book useful insights not commonly found in programming books, and will make it especially easy for a beginner to pick up and learn.

Learn by building real apps.

We believe that the best way to learn something new is to put it into practice. By the end of the course you’ll have more than just experience, you will have working examples of apps you’ve built.

We’ll turn the popular web App Stand To Make into a native application.

Both of these apps will be built, polished, and ready for release by the end of the book.

Programming is like building with Lego, each brick fits into another in exactly the right way. Once you understand how to put the pieces together you won't have anything to stop you.
